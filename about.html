About Us

Your trusted on-campus delivery partner. Fast, secure, and reliable.

Our Mission

Pacy Parcels was founded to revolutionize on-campus logistics. Our mission is to provide an efficient and affordable peer-to-peer delivery network, ensuring all parcels and documents reach their destination securely and on time. We aim to enhance the campus experience by eliminating logistical hassles for students and faculty.

Our Services

Leveraging a robust digital platform, Pacy Parcels offers a seamless delivery experience. Users can place orders, track real-time parcel movements, and receive instant notifications. Our network of verified campus personnel ensures swift, reliable, and contact-free delivery, available daily from 3PM to 9 PM.

Core Values

We are committed to Integrity, ensuring all transactions are transparent and trustworthy. Our focus on Efficiency guarantees a streamlined delivery process from request to completion. Above all, we prioritize Security, employing rigorous protocols to protect every parcel entrusted to us.
